About Us

Our Story

Our name LearnYuva conveys our dedication to bring a healthy transition in the life of the young and the curious. Our name defines our noble profession, "Learn" which means “to gain knowledge” and "Yuva" symbolizing “the youth”. LearnYuva is here to boost the thinking capacity of young and brilliant minds by building an indivisible bond between knowledge and youth through live and interactive sessions.

Our Vision

We, at LearnYuva, strive to provide students with an environment conducive to innovation and learning with dedicated faculties to help students realize their potential. From the portals at LearnYuva, we intend to send forth sensible and socially conscious individuals who shall be the instruments of positive change and peace in this strife-stricken world.

Our Goal

We aim to reach each and every student who is struggling to learn through conventional methods by equipping them with a massive range of technical and non-technical courses like app development, foreign languages, robotics, etc. with insightful learning experiences thus ensuring their all-round development.Our team is clear on its goal and comprises of like-minded individuals who believe in educating for a better tomorrow.
